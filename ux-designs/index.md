## Notes
- Services are always linked to **Policies** and instantiated via **Forums**.  
- A **Stakeholder** (person, org, IoT, API, AI agent) can act as:  
  - Policy Manager  
  - Service Provider  
  - Forum Participant  
  - Policy Consumer  
- Payments are enabled via **Stripe** integration for direct fees or subscriptions.
